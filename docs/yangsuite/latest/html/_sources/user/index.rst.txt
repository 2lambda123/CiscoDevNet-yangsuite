Welcome to YANG Suite!
======================

YANG Suite is a set of tools related to YANG models (`RFC 6020`_, `RFC 7950`_)
and related technologies such as NETCONF (`RFC 6241`_).
It provides a modular infrastructure atop which various application plugins
can be used.

Please subscribe to yang-suite-users@cisco.com for announcements.

Getting Started
---------------

If this is your first time running YANG Suite, you have two ways to begin:

1. Configure YANG Suite to `connect to a device <../ysdevices/>`_
   (router or switch) and then
   `download supported YANG models from the device <../ysfilemanager/download_schemas/>`_.
2. `Upload YANG model files <../ysfilemanager/upload_schemas/>`_
   from your computer to YANG Suite.

Once you have provided YANG models to YANG Suite by either of the above
methods, you can then proceed to
`define sets of YANG models <../ysfilemanager/define_yangset/>`_
of interest.

Once this is done you can proceed to learn how to
`explore the contents of these YANG models <../ysyangtree/>`_
or (if yangsuite-netconf is installed)
`use these models with NETCONF <../ysnetconf/>`_.

For more information on any of these topics, use the table of contents
or the search box, both on the right side of this page.
To return to this page at any time, use the "Help" menu in the
sidebar to the left of any YANG Suite page. For page-specific help,
click the ``(?)`` icon in the top right corner.

About YANG Suite
----------------

The core application plugins for YANG Suite are:

`yangsuite-filemanager <../ysfilemanager/>`_
  Manages storage of YANG schema files within the application.
  Allows users to upload YANG schema files to the application.

`yangsuite-devices <../ysdevices/>`_
  Manages device profile definitions and access to devices.

`yangsuite-yangtree <../ysyangtree/>`_
  Provides basic browsing of YANG models in a tree format.


Additional (optional) application plugins include:

`yangsuite-netconf <../ysnetconf/>`_
  Download supported YANG models from a device,
  construct and send NETCONF (`RFC 6241`_) RPCs to a device.

User Administration
-------------------

From the menu bar at the top of any YANG Suite page,
select `"Admin → Manage users" </admin/>`_.
In the page that loads, you can manage users
and groups to access the YANG Suite server.

Troubleshooting
---------------

You can browse the server logs generated by YANG Suite by selecting
`"Admin → View logs" </yangsuite/logs/>`_ from the menu at the top of any page.

If you face any other issues, please email us at yang-suite-support@cisco.com.
